# Number Match

Number match is a simple game where you must match numbers together so that they either add up to 10 (e.g. 2 and 8), or are duplicates of themself (2 and 2). This can be up down left, right, or wrapped around to the next line.

This is a popular mobile game, but most on the market today are full of annoying ads and pop ups that are not only inconvenient, but could be a security risk. As well as being set out to made money.

This app has none of that, just clean uninterrupted gameplay. With Material Dynamic theming so it always looks good.
