## Task Description
I created an Android Application to provide accurate, and precise weather forecasting using common APIs such as Open Weather's OneCall 3.0 API, as well as Google Maps SDK to display weather radar maps. UI design was also an important consideration, being designed in an accessible way that suits many different people's needs, with customisation features. Finally, I wrote up a report on the overall development process, and my findings. What went well and what could be improved
